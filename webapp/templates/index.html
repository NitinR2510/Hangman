Let's play Hangman with CNTK!

Choose a word that you would like the trained model to attempt to guess. Make sure your word consists only of letters: no punctuation, spaces, or other symbols allowed! No need to reveal the word to us -- you'll provide feedback on each guess, just like in a real game of Hangman -- but we do need to know how long the word is, so that we can include the right number of blank spaces.
